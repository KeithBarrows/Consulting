---
description: Evaluate a job offer and generate a polite salary negotiation script
---

This workflow evaluates a job offer against market data and creates a counter-offer script based on skills.

1. Ask the USER for the target [Role], [Company], base salary [Amount], the target job description, and the USER's resume/skills.
2. Execute the following instruction:
"I have received a job offer for [Role] at [Company] with a base salary of [Amount]. Based on this job description, what is a realistic market range? Provide 3 justifications for a higher offer based on my skills, and a polite script to counter."
3. Present the market range, justifications, and negotiation script to the USER.
4. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
