---
description: Discover alternative job titles and hidden roles based on skills
---

This workflow helps uncover unexpected job opportunities that match a skill set.

1. Ask the USER for a list of their skills and their career preferences (e.g., higher pay, better work-life balance, remote work).
2. Act as the Job Search Agent and execute the following instruction:
"Based on my skills in [List Skills], suggest 5 alternative job titles I might not have considered but would be qualified for, focusing on roles with [Preferences]."
3. Evaluate the suggestions and present them to the USER.
4. Ensure the suggestions are realistic and not hallucinated.
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
