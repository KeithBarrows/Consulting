description: Transform a resume summary into a compelling professional narrative for the LinkedIn About section
---

This workflow helps write a strong, strategic, storytelling-focused LinkedIn About section.

1. Review the USER's profile, positions, and skills directly from the CSV files in `C:\Repos\Consulting\LinkedIn` to extract their key achievements and unique value proposition.
2. Ask the USER for their target [Area], career direction, and their [Target Role].
2. Execute the following instruction:
"Write a 3-paragraph LinkedIn 'About' section that highlights my achievements in [Area], my unique value proposition, and my career direction.
Para 1: A hook summarizing my journey and current expertise.
Para 2: 2-3 key achievements (quantified) and core competencies, tailored to a [Target Role].
Para 3: My professional mission, what I’m looking to solve next, and a call-to-action to connect.
Tone: Confident, strategic, and professional".

3. Remind the USER to verify the output and ensure it represents their true achievements.
4. Present the generated About section to the USER.
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
