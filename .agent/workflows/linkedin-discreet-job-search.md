---
description: Configure LinkedIn for a discreet job search to alert recruiters without tipping off your current employer
---

This workflow provides a step-by-step guide to setting up LinkedIn for job searching securely and effectively.

### 1. Turn Off Activity Notifications
Before making any edits to your profile, ensure your network won't be alerted to your changes.
- Go to **Settings > Visibility > "Share profile updates"** and toggle it **OFF**.

### 2. Discreet "Open to Work" Configuration
To avoid the green banner while still alerting recruiters, update your settings as follows:
- Click on your profile photo.
- Select **"Open to" > "Finding a new job"**.
- Fill in job titles and locations.
- **CRUCIAL STEP:** Under "Visibility," select **"Recruiters only"**. This ensures the green "Open to Work" frame is NOT applied, which is key to discretion.

### 3. General Best Practices
As you update your profile, prioritize the following:
- **Use Action Verbs:** Use words like led, spearheaded, developed, optimized, negotiated.
- **Quantify Everything:** Use numbers, percentages, and dollar amounts to demonstrate impact.

Once your settings are configured, you can utilize the other LinkedIn Workflows (e.g., Headline Optimization, About Section) safely.
