description: Write optimized, keyword-rich LinkedIn headline options
---

This workflow helps create a compelling, keyword-rich LinkedIn headline to attract recruiters and highlight value.

1. Review the USER's recent experience and skills directly from the files in `C:\Repos\Consulting\LinkedIn` (such as `Profile.csv`, `Positions.csv`, and `Skills.csv`). Extract their [Current/Past Title] and identify 2-3 key competencies.
2. Ask the USER for their [Target Title], [Industry] sector, and if they prefer the Standard Headline or the "Open to Conversation" headline.
3. Execute the following instruction depending on their choice:

**Option 1: Standard Headline Optimization**
"Act as a LinkedIn career coach. Write 5 headline options for a [Current/Past Title] looking to move into a [Target Title] role in the [Industry] sector. Include top-searched keywords, 2-3 key competencies, and a value proposition. Keep them under 220 characters and avoid generic 'passionate' language. Structure: Title | Key Skill 1 | Key Skill 2 | Value-Driven Keyword".

**Option 2: Open to Conversation**
"Rewrite my headline to suggest I am open to new opportunities without using the words 'seeking' or 'actively looking.' Use a value-focused approach like: '[Current Title] | Specializing in [X] and [Y] | Open to new strategic leadership roles'".

4. Present the headline options to the USER.
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
