This workflow uses the Resume Expert Agent to rework past role bullets to focus on measurable accomplishments.

1. If you don't already have them, ask the USER to provide the list of bullet points from their past roles.
2. Act as the Resume Expert Agent and execute the following instruction:
"Rework my past role bullets to focus on measurable accomplishments, not just responsibilities. Use this format: 'Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]'. Here are my bullets: [Paste Bullet Points]."
3. Present the rewritten bullets to the USER.
4. Verify the output to ensure no experience or metrics were hallucinated (invented).
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
