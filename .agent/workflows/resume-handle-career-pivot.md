---
description: Handle career pivots by highlighting transferable skills
---

This workflow aids in transitioning between career fields by mapping transferable skills.

1. Ask the USER for their [Old Field], [New Field], current [Job Title], and their current experience/resume.
2. Act as the Resume Expert Agent and execute the following instruction:
"I am transitioning from [Old Field] to [New Field]. Based on my experience as a [Job Title], list 5 transferable skills in business language and explain how they map to a [New Field] role."
3. Present the list and explanations to the USER.
4. Ensure no experience was hallucinated.
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
