description: Rewrite LinkedIn experience bullets to focus on results and impact
---

This workflow converts task-based duties into results-driven impact statements for the LinkedIn Experience section.

1. Review the USER's past roles and current bullet points describing those roles directly from `C:\Repos\Consulting\LinkedIn\Positions.csv`.
2. Ask the USER for the specific [Job Title] they want to optimize and the [Industry].
2. Execute the following instruction:
"Rewrite my [Job Title] experience bullets to be more impactful. Focus on results and impact rather than just tasks. Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) and incorporate keywords that recruiters in [Industry] search for. Here is my current bullet: [Paste old description]".

Example Output Goal: Instead of "Managed a team," use "Led a team of 5 to increase sales by 20% in 6 months."

3. Remind the USER of these LinkedIn best practices:
- **Use Action Verbs:** Use words like led, spearheaded, developed, optimized, negotiated.
- **Quantify Everything:** Use numbers, percentages, and dollar amounts to demonstrate impact.

4. Present the rewritten bullets to the USER.
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
