description: Research a company and provide key interview talking points
---

This workflow acts as a market researcher to gather insights and talking points for a target company.

1. Ask the USER for the [Company Name] they are interviewing with or applying to.
2. Act as the Job Search Agent/Market Researcher and execute the following instruction:
"Act as a market researcher. Analyze [Company Name] based on their recent news, mission, and company culture. Provide 3 key talking points I can use in an interview to show I understand their challenges."
3. Gather the required information through research if needed (e.g., using web search tools to find recent news).
4. Present the analysis and talking points to the USER.
5. Ask the USER, "What is missing to make this 10/10?" and iterate on the feedback.
